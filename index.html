About Waft Wave

Waft Wave is a browser-based toolkit for Elektron Monomachine mk2 DigiPRO waveforms: build, audition, import/export, and send/receive over MIDI SysEx.

It also sends Machinedrum SDS and can export WAV files for your sampler.

Thanks to JohnnyW for testing and steer towards the wave chain engines.
